Key Trends - What Worked in 2025

Human Touch Over AI Hype

The most celebrated work showed technology amplifying, not replacing, human insight. In a world saturated with AI content, the human touch became the differentiator.

Compound Consistent Creativity

Brands that stick with creative platforms over years achieve 4x more ROI. Consistency beats novelty. The biggest winners ran platforms for 10, 20, and 65 years.

Humor as Performance Driver

72% of people would choose a brand that uses humor over a competitor. Amusing ads trigger brain reward systems, making messages more memorable and distinctive.

Creator & Fan-Led Creativity

Creator-led campaigns dominated. Brands that gave creators freedom to connect with their own audiences saw the strongest results across social and digital.
